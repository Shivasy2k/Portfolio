Sivakumar Dhanasekar is a software engineering leader and enterprise architect with more than two decades of experience building enterprise-scale applications across financial technology, retail, identity, fraud, and supply-chain domains.

His work focuses on AI-driven, cloud-native, and data-centric platforms that combine secure architecture, scalable microservices, API orchestration, and practical automation. At Equifax, he leads digital transformation initiatives for identity and fraud services, including AI-based solutions that support device reputation, fraud risk scoring, trust scoring, and verification confidence.

Sivakumar has deep hands-on experience across Java, Spring Boot, REST APIs, cloud platforms, Kubernetes, Docker, CI/CD, and modern application modernization. He has led distributed engineering teams, mentored developers, owned technical design and delivery quality, and guided complex migrations from legacy systems to secure cloud-ready platforms.

Beyond delivery leadership, he contributes to the broader technology community through IEEE and IETE memberships, technical program committees, peer review service, scholarly publications, conference talks, and applied research in trustworthy AI, fintech, fraud detection, and cloud-native platforms.
